=== CAMERA MOVEMENT CONTROLS ===.
UP/DOWN/RIGHT/LEFT arrows - Rotate camera.
WASD - Move camera. 
Left Mouse Btn - Pan.
Right Mouse Btn / Mouse Scroll - Zoom.
K/L - Roll camera. Camera rotates around its look vector.

===LIGHTING CONTROLS===.

On/Off Keys.
1 - Wireframe Key.

Not implemented.
2 - Directional Light Keys.
3 - Point Light Keys.
4 - Spot Light Keys.
